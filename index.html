Meet The Team

Junaid Shah

Junaid completed his KPMG sponsored degree in 2019 and qualified as a chartered accountant in 2020. He has spent 6 years in audit and is currently working in M&A at KPMG, as well as Treasurer of the Muslim Network.

Ambar Khan

Ambar started her career at HSBC as a Business Manager with 4 years experience in helping clients expand. She is also an International Business Graduate with extensive experience working in China.

Qasim Iqbal

Qasim began his career at RSM in audit for 5 years and also participated in the Diana Award programme as a mentor. He then transferred to an M&A boutique in 2020 and is currently working at PwC in M&A.

Jehona Hashani

Jehona holds a BA from King's College London and MA from the University of Warwick in History. She began her career at KPMG and has spent 3 years in audit while working towards the ACA qualification.

Jumanah Kasujee

Jumanah began her career at KPMG, spending 5 years in audit alongside completing her degree in Accounting. She is also Secretary of the Muslim Network at KPMG and involved in various mentoring schemes.
